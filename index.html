PIG DICE RULES

  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice until a 1 is rolled or player decides to hold and pass their turn
  • The number rolled is added to the temporary score unless a player rolls 1.
  • If the player rolls a 1, he or she loses their temporary score and the turn is passed to the other player.
  • The temproray score is added to the results when the user decides to pass their turn to the next player.
  • The first player to gain 100 points is the winner.
